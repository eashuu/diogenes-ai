# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

* Using welcoming and inclusive language
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
* Focusing on what is best for the community
* Showing empathy towards other community members
* Engaging in thoughtful and civil discourse
* Acknowledging when you are wrong and apologizing

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

* The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
* Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without explicit permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
* Deliberate misinformation or spreading false information
* Exclusionary language or gatekeeping

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers at [maintainer-email]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

### Consequences

Violations of the Code of Conduct may result in:

1. **Warning**: A private message explaining the violation and expected behavior
2. **Temporary Ban**: Removal from project activities for a specified period
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the project community

The severity of consequences will be determined based on:
* Nature and severity of the violation
* Pattern of behavior
* Impact on the community
* Intent and responsiveness to feedback

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

* Issue trackers and pull requests
* Discussion forums
* Project documentation
* Code repositories
* Social media channels related to the project
* Project events and meetings
* Any other spaces managed by the project team

It also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.
